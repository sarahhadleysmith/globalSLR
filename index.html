Welcome to Global SLR

Goal: Keep your village alive.

The rising sea level will move closer to your villager's homes each turn.

The locations of villager's homes are indicated with orange boxes.
The locations of gardens are indicated with green boxes.

Completely build a wall to stop the rising sea.

You can not initally choose the location of the wall, but once you start building you can shift the wall to the left or right, for a price.
Plant mangroves to decrease the speed of the sea level rise, for a price.
If all of your villager's homes flood, you lose.
If your food level reaches 0, you lose.
